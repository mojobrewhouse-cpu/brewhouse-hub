The Vibe

Where Santa Barbara Watches the World Cup

Doors open early. Big screens fired up inside. Cold craft beer in your hand, smell of burgers and fish tacos off the grill, and a bar full of strangers yelling at the same screen when somebody finally scores. That's match day at The Brewhouse. No cover. No reservations needed. Just show up.

☕ Not drinking? Grab an espresso, cold brew, or pastry from Brewhouse Coffee right on property — open daily from 7 AM. Same building, same matches, no pint required.

🌿 Not into soccer? Our patios stay open all day — perfect spot to watch the watchers, eat lunch in the sun, and dip in for a goal if the crowd roars loud enough.
