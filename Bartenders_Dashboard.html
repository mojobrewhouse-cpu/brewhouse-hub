Setup Instructions ▶
  1. Download "DIRECTV FOR BUSINESS Remote" from App Store
  2. Make sure iPad is on the same WiFi as the receivers
  3. Log in with your DirecTV business account
  4. App should auto-detect receivers on the network
  5. Name each TV (e.g. "Bar TV 1", "Patio TV")
  6. Group TVs to change multiple channels at once
ℹ️ If app doesn't find receivers: They may need to be network-connected. Call DirecTV Business at 888-388-4249 and ask about DECA adapters for iPad control.
What You Can Control ▶

Channels: Change any TV from anywhere in the restaurant

Grouping: Switch all bar TVs to the same game at once

Sports Schedule: See all upcoming games in the app

Name TVs: Label each receiver so you know which is which

Volume: Use Samsung TV remotes or SmartThings for volume

🔊 Open Sonos

If app not installed, download from App Store

Sonos Tips

🎵 Group Rooms: Play the same music in multiple zones

🔇 Volume Limits: Set max volume per zone to protect speakers

Suggested Playlists

🌙 Late Night Energy – Electronic, deep house, downtempo beats
